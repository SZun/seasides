Seaside's Togo delivers a taste of summer picnics from the east coast to sweet home Chicago. Inspired by our founder, Bill Nevruz’s memories of his childhood vacations on the jersey shore, this unique menu offers crispy fried chicken, slow-cooked ribs & fresh seafood dinners designed for you to enjoy with your family & friends. Roll up your sleeves and indulge!
